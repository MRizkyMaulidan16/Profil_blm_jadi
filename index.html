Lawang Sewu Building

Apart from the unique building design, Lawang Sewu has stained glass ornaments manufactured by Johannes Lourens Schouten. The stained glass tells stories about the prosperity and beauty of Java, Dutch rule over Semarang and Batavia, maritime cities and the glory of the railway.
